About LAB211

Lab211 Design Studios was founded in 2013 by James Bagley and Marcus Gunn. They first began working together as interns on various projects at Bowie State University in 2005, maintaining contact throughout college and their professional careers. Both, having aspirations to become entrepreneurs and a passion for creative web design, decided to go into business. When determining the name for the company, the two agreed the name should represent the where they first started, which was LAB #211 in the CS building at their university, hence the name.
